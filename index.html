Cat breeds I will get one day and why

  1. Maine coon

    2. There are many reasons for one to get a cat of the maine coon breed, the most important being its size. Maine coon cats are the biggest domesticated cat breed today. They are also recommended to beginner owners, so there is no need to worry if a person is capable of caring for it. They come in many different colours, ensuring a broad colour pool to pick from. Maine coons seem like the perfect cat breed, however, there is one problem. The maine coon cat breed is not a usual cat breed you can find in the Czech republic :(
    Of course a person can find a place to get them, but price is directly corelated to demand, and thus they cost a lot of money. You cannot find them in animal shelters, unless they are old and ill. Although adoption is a great thing to do, I do not have the money to care for an old ill cat, that has special needs and only a few months left to live.
